# Privacy Policy — Judgy Calculator

_Last updated: July 20, 2026_

Judgy Calculator does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. Period.

- **No data collection.** The extension has no analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.
- **No network activity.** It makes zero network requests. All code, fonts, and artwork
  ship inside the extension package, and every calculation happens locally in your browser.
- **No storage.** Nothing you type is saved anywhere — not even locally. Close the popup
  and your arithmetic history is gone.
- **No permissions.** The extension requests no browser permissions and cannot read your
  browsing activity, tabs, or any website content.
- **No third parties.** No data is sold or shared with anyone, because none exists.

The sun's judgement of your arithmetic is rendered locally and is never recorded.
